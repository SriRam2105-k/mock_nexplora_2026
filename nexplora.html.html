Structure

Event Format

The journey from concept to code — mapped out in three definitive phases.

Step 01
Online Idea Submission
Register your team and submit your initial problem-solving idea or abstract before the registration deadline to secure a spot.
Step 02
Shortlisting Round
Submitted ideas are reviewed by a panel, and the most promising and innovative teams are selected to compete in the onsite sprint.
Step 03
24-Hour Onsite Hackathon
The main event begins. An intense 24-hour sprint where you code, build, and deploy a working prototype with guidance from dedicated mentors.
